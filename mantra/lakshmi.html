Lakshmi mantras

List of some mantras dedicated to Lakshmi.

1. Mahalakshmi Mantra

Om Sarvabaadhaa Vinirmukto, Dhan Dhaanyah Sutaanvitah | Manushyo Matprasaaden Bhavishyati Na Sanshayah Om ||

2. Lakshmi Gayatri Mantra

Om Mahadevyai Cha Vidmahe Vishnu Patnayai Cha Dheemahi, Tanno Lakshmih Prachodyaat ||

3. Lakshmi Bija Mantra

Om Hreem Shreem Lakshmibhayo Namah॥

4. Shree Lakshmi Nrisingh Mantra

|| Om Hring Kshraung Shring Lakshmi Nrisinghay Namah || || Om Kling Kshraung Shring Lakshmi Devyai Namah ||

5. Mahalakshmi Yakshineevidya

||Om Hring kling MahaLakshmyai Namah ||

6. Lakshmi Mantra

Om Shring Hring Kling Tribhuvan Mahalakshmyai Asmaakam Daaridray Naashay Prachur Dhan Dehi Dehi Kling Hring Shring Om ।

7. Dwadashakshar Mahalakshmi Mantra

|| Om Aing Hring Shring Kling Sauh Jagatprasutyai Namah ||
