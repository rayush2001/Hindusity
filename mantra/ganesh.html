Ganesh mantra

List of some Ganesh mantras

1. Ganesh Beej Mantra

"Om Gam Ganpataye Namah"

2. Ganesh Gayatri Mantra

“Aum Ekadantaya Viddhamahe, Vakratundaya Dhimahi, Tanno Danti Prachodayat॥”

3. The Rinn Harta Mantra

"Om Ganesh Rinnam Chhindhi Varenyam Hoong Namaah Phutt"

4. Namavali Mantras

i. “Om Ganadhyakhsaya Namah” ii. “Om Gajananaya Namah” iii. ”Om Vignanaashnay Namah” iv. “Om Lambodaraya Namah” v. “Om Sumukhaya Namah” vi. “Om Gajkarnikaya Namah” vii. “Om Vikataya Namah” viii. ”Om Vinayakaya Namah”

5. The Shaktivinayak Mantra

“Om Hreeng Greeng Hreeng”

6. Ganesh Mool Mantra

“Om Shreem Hreem Kleem Glaum Gam Ganapataye Vara Varad Sarvajan janmay Vashamanaye Swaha Tatpurushaye Vidmahe Vakratundaye Dhimahi Tanno Danti Prachodyat Om Shantih Shantih Shantihi”
